Who we are

Boulder Climb starts with a group of people who loves bouldering and rock climbing and wishes to spread the joy to people who are interested in it. We would love to teach beginners the ropes and hope to make it easy for them that they don't feel burdened at all. We also have beginner to advance level courses for both rock and bouldering.
